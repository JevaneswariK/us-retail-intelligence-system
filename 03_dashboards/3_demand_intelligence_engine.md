# 📊 Demand Intelligence Engine

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze category-level performance, efficiency, and demand distribution to identify strong, weak, and volatile segments in the retail market.

---

## 📌 Key KPIs
- Average Revenue per Category
- Revenue Dispersion (CV)
- Downside Risk (P10 Revenue)
- Revenue Efficiency Ratio

---

## 📈 Charts
- Category Performance Segmentation
- Revenue Momentum Distribution
- Seasonal Volatility Patterns
- Revenue Inequality (Lorenz Curve)

## 🧠 Business Understanding (VERY IMPORTANT)

This dashboard focuses on **how revenue is distributed across categories and how stable each category is over time**.

### 🔍 Key Business Questions Answered:
- Are revenues concentrated in a few categories or evenly distributed?
- Which categories are stable vs highly volatile?
- What is the downside risk during weak months?
- Which categories are efficient performers vs inconsistent ones?

---

### 💡 Core Insights:

#### 1. Revenue Distribution Insight
- Revenue is **not equally distributed**
- A few categories contribute **major share → market concentration**
- Verified using **Lorenz Curve (inequality analysis)**

#### 2. Stability vs Variability
- Categories are segmented into:
  - Stable Performers → low risk, consistent demand
  - Balanced Performers → moderate stability
  - High Variability → unpredictable demand

👉 Helps businesses decide:
- Where to invest
- Where to reduce risk exposure

---

#### 3. Downside Risk (P10)
- Shows **worst-case monthly revenue scenario**
- Important for:
  - Financial planning
  - Risk management

---

#### 4. Efficiency Insight
- Measures how top-performing categories outperform the median
- High ratio = strong leaders dominating market

---

#### 5. Momentum Behavior
- Demand changes are:
  - Positive → growth periods
  - Negative → contraction
  - Neutral → stable demand

👉 Helps detect:
- Demand cycles
- Seasonal effects
- Market instability

---

## 🚀 Final Takeaway
The Demand Intelligence Engine transforms raw sales data into **strategic category insights**, helping businesses:

- Identify **high-performing categories**
- Detect **risk-prone segments**
- Understand **market inequality**
- Optimize **resource allocation**
